Hello World!

You have found yourself inside the online nexus of Thomas James Alexander’s digital memory banks.

Thomas was educated by North Carolina State University (BAE Dept rankings) as a Biological Engineer with a concentration in Bioprocessing.

A Biological Engineer’s training is a rigorous as any other engineer but with the exception that we are trained to design devices that cater to the needs and exploit the benefits of living systems.

This website serves as a second brain focused on collecting/connecting resources and researchers in Synthetic Biology and related fields.

Synthetic Biology, which refers to the application of the engineering principles of abstraction and standardization to biological (living) systems, seeks to reverse engineer the molecular machinery of life to solve human problems.
